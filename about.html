What is Dix Consulting?

Dix Consulting is a service that manages your automated QA. We create and maintain your end-to-end tests, as well as integrate them into your development process. You can think of us as an expert in-house team of QA Engineers, without the hassle of hiring and managing them.

Who is Dix Consulting for?

Dix Consulting is for technology companies that need help with automated Quality Assurance. We work best with companies that do not yet have automated end-to-end tests or are unhappy with their existing tests. If you have a question about whether Dix Consulting is right for you, please contact us.

What test do we do?

Dix Consulting manages automated end-to-end testing. This means we create automated test scripts to use your application as a customer would. For example, an e-commerce site would test that customers can add an item to the cart and check out. We do not do manual QA, as we want to focus on areas where we can provide unique value.

Why not have my developers manage automated QA?

Dix Consulting works well for teams whose developers do not have time to learn an automated test framework and create tests. In our experience, companies prefer to use developer time for product improvements rather than for writing tests. If you decide later to have your developer's own QA, we provide a great foundation for them to build on.

Does it work for my front-end framework (React, Vue, Angular, Svelte, etc.)?

Dix consulting can test any application that is accessible over the web. This includes applications built with various front-end frameworks like React, Vue, or Angular.
